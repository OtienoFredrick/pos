Attendance & Participation Standards

Online Session Requirements

  • Students must join online sessions at least 5 minutes before the scheduled start time to ensure technical readiness
  • Late arrivals (more than 10 minutes) may not be admitted to live sessions and will need to access recorded materials
  • Consistent attendance is essential for reading skill development and comprehension improvement
  • Students are expected to participate actively in reading discussions and comprehension activities

Engagement Expectations

  • Complete all assigned reading exercises between sessions
  • Participate in online discussion forums related to reading materials
  • Submit comprehension assessments within specified timeframes
  • Maintain regular communication with instructors regarding reading progress

Registration & Enrollment Process

Comprehensive Registration Requirements

All prospective students must complete a detailed online registration via kyasacademy.com

Registration includes a reading skills assessment to determine appropriate program level

Students must provide:

  • Accurate personal and contact information
  • Educational background and reading goals
  • Previous reading support or literacy program experience
  • Technology requirements verification for online learning

Enrollment Confirmation

Upon successful registration, students receive a comprehensive welcome package including:

  • A free online website for reading and math materials
  • Personalized schedule with dates and times
  • Instructor contact information and office hours schedule
  • Guidelines and tips for making the most of the course and achieving learning goals.

Financial Policies & Payment Structure

Payment Options

  • Multiple payment methods accepted: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, American Express

Learning Materials & Educational Resource

Required Reading Materials Provided by the instructor

  • Digital Resources: Provided by the instructor and additional free online reading platforms
  • Supplementary Materials: Age-appropriate reading selections tailored to individual comprehension levels
  • Assessment Tools: Regular reading fluency and comprehension evaluation materials

Academic Integrity & Code of Conduct

Professional Learning Environment

  • Respectful Communication: All interactions between students, instructors, and support staff maintain professional courtesy
  • Academic Honesty: Original work required for all reading assignments and comprehension assessments
  • Collaborative Learning: Encouraged peer support while maintaining individual accountability for reading progress
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Inclusive environment welcoming diverse backgrounds and reading experiences

Disciplinary Procedures

  • Warning System: Progressive approach to address conduct concerns with reading support and additional resources
  • Removal Policy: Serious violations may result in program suspension without tuition refund
  • Appeal Process: Students may request review of disciplinary decisions through our academic affairs department
  • Supportive Intervention: Focus on reading success with additional support before punitive measures

Privacy Protection & Data Security

Student Information Management

  • FERPA Compliance: Educational records protected according to federal privacy regulations
  • Limited Data Collection: Only information necessary for reading program delivery and academic support
  • Secure Platform: Encrypted online learning environment protecting student reading progress and personal information
  • Third-Party Restrictions: No sharing of student data without explicit written consent

Technology & Platform Security

  • Regular Security Updates: Continuous platform monitoring and security enhancement
  • Student Data Rights: Full access to personal reading progress records and communication history
  • Retention Policy: Academic records maintained for educational continuity and transcript services
  • Privacy Training: All staff receive ongoing training in student privacy protection and data handling
